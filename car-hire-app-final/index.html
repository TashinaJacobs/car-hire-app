Top Spots In July

Lake Taupo, New Zealand
Lake Taupo is a lake in the North Island of New Zealand. It is in the caldera of the Taupo Volcano. With a surface area of 616 square kilometres, it is the largest lake by surface area in New Zealand!
Kerikeri, New Zealand
Kerikeri is a North Island road trip that no tourist can ignore. When leaving Auckland you can choose to take the Northern Gateway Toll Road, which provides easy access to the riverside village of Puhoi.
Napier, New Zealand
Napier, a coastal city on New Zealand's North Island, is set amid the renowned wine-producing region of Hawke's Bay. Rebuilt after a 1931 earthquake, the city is known for art deco landmarks.
